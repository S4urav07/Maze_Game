Bear's Journey Home

This is a story of two bears who were lost in maze . There are guards who are guarding the ways to home .In the way you can find apples 🍎 which will give you 10 points each. Help the bears to go to their home without getting caught by guards . If bears intersect with guards the game is Over. So be careful out there.

How to control

  • To move brown bear 🐻 you can use key W to go Up, key S to go Down, key A to go Left and Key D to go Right
  • To move brown pink bear 👚🐻 you can use key ↑ to go Up, key ↓ to go Down, key ← to go Left and Key →to go Right
  • Game is finished when two bear will intersect with each other.

Don't Give up and Help them to find their home .

It's only you who can help them .

Have a Great Time.
